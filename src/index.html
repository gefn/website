Dynamic, synergetic processes improving resource efficiencies and binding CO2

Gefn's technology for production and application of renewable specialty chemicals, solvents and intermediates offers sustainable and CO2 binding processes for localised valorisation of by-products and wastes from processing of oleo-feedstocks, and CO2 from carbon-neutral processes and air.

Flexible process technology

Gefn’s process intensification-based technology is applied as complete and modular plug-on processes or in separate units. The modularity minimises downtime during integration and enables modifications and even relocation in a simple manner. The dynamic nature of Gefn’s processes and equipment allows flexibility in raw materials and products for optimisation of production costs and revenues, and gives faster responses to the needs of customers and the market.

Efficient replacement of fossil-based specialty chemicals and solvents

The green chemicals obtained by Gefn’s technology are suitable for replacing fossil-based specialty chemicals and solvents in wide range of industrial and consumer products. Gefn develops optimised formulations maximising performances and minimising costs and environmental effects of the end products.

Tomorrow’s chemical industry

Gefn’s technology enables profitable utilisation of sustainable renewable feedstocks and CO2. The company is constantly developing its technology towards yielding green chemicals having constantly higher proportion of their chemical structures containing carbon atoms originated from direct utilisation of CO2.

What is Gefn?

Gefn is an innovative Icelandic chemical engineering company developing and commercialising innovative solutions for production and application of green chemicals and providing technical services in related fields.
